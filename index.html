Frequently Asked Questions

What is your pricing model for private monitor access?

We offer private monitors in packages of 15 events for $200 monthly, with a maximum of 45 per user ($600/m)

Can you explain the "Early Notification" feature more in-depth?

Our monitoring system enables members to receive notifications of up to one minute in advance about upcoming inventory additions. This feature empowers our members to proactively prepare for restocks, ensuring they are well-prepared to act swiftly when the second notification confirms the availability of stock on-site.

What is available to users who decide to not additionally purchase the private monitor subscription?

All regular members enjoy access to the most popular event channels. Additionally, if a significant number of users add the same event to their private channels, it will be moved to the general member channels. We also provide a section where users can initiate polls for events to be included in the global monitoring system, and if there is sufficient demand, these events will be added.
